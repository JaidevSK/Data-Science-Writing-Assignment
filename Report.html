References

  • Sub-Divisional Monthly Rainfall (1901-2017). (n.d.). data.gov.in. Retrieved from https://www.data.gov.in/resource/sub-divisional-monthly-rainfall-1901-2017.

    This dataset provides monthly rainfall data for various sub-divisions in India from 1901 to 2017. It includes information on the geographical location of the sub-divisions and the amount of rainfall recorded each month. This dataset can be used to analyze rainfall patterns and trends over time, and to study the impact of rainfall on agriculture and other sectors.

  • Gupta, A. (n.d.). Crop Yield in Indian States Dataset. Kaggle. Retrieved from https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/akshatgupta7/crop-yield-in-indian-states-dataset.

    This dataset contains information about crop yield in various Indian states. It includes data on the types of crops grown, the area harvested, and the yield per hectare. This dataset can be used to study the factors that affect crop yield, such as climate, soil type, and agricultural practices.

  • Seasonal and Annual Mean Temperature Series (Period 1901-2021). (n.d.). data.gov.in. Retrieved from https://www.data.gov.in/resource/seasonal-and-annual-mean-temperature-series-period-1901-2021.

    This dataset provides seasonal and annual mean temperature data for India from 1901 to 2021. It includes information on the geographical location of the weather stations and the mean temperature recorded each season and year. This dataset can be used to analyze temperature trends over time, and to study the impact of climate change on India.
